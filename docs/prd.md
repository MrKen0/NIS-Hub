# NIS Hub PRD (Product Requirements Document)

## Objective
Deliver a mobile-first community hub for Naijas in Stevenage that allows structured discovery of services, events, requests, and announcements while preserving WhatsApp as the live conversation channel.

## Target audience
- Nigerian expats and students in Stevenage
- community organizers, small business owners, service providers
- volunteers and grassroots event hosts

## Scope
- Auth (Firebase Auth) + role model
- Service listing + search
- Request board (ask/offer)
- Event publishing + RSVP
- Community notice feed
- Admin moderation workflows
- PWA installable and offline-capable read access

## Success metrics
- 70% of active users complete one action (post/engage) in first week
- 80% of reports reviewed in 24 hours
- <5% of users receive trust/role throttling after 30 days

## Out of scope (MVP)
- payments / escrow
- in-app chat (WhatsApp integration only)
- advanced recommender engine
